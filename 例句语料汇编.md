# 🌸 Topic 1 · My Favourite Season {#topic1}

**年份**：2023仿写 | **主题**：描写最喜欢的季节及原因

## Opening 开篇句

> Different people like different seasons.

## Sentence 2 · 主题句

📌 **写作指导**：点明最喜欢的季节 + 一个核心感受词（refreshing / lively / cosy）。避免只说 "I like spring"，需加 because 或 for me 引入原因。

**A 版**

> To me, spring stands out clearly above all other seasons, filling my heart with excitement and energy every single year.

**B 版**

> My favourite season is spring, which always greets me with warm breezes, blooming gardens, and a fresh sense of hope.

---

## Sentence 3 · 景色描写

📌 **写作指导**：用2-3个感官细节（视觉/嗅觉/触觉）描绘该季节自然景象。运用 there be 或 when 状语从句增加层次感。

**A 版**

> Walking gently through the park, you can vividly see cherry blossoms dancing in the warm breeze and smell fresh flowers everywhere.

**B 版**

> Everywhere you look, pink and white blossoms cover the trees like a soft blanket, which makes the whole world feel gentle and alive.

---

## Sentence 4 · 户外活动

📌 **写作指导**：写一项该季节特有的户外活动，加频率副词（often/always）和具体地点，使场景真实可信。

**A 版**

> I often fly kites enthusiastically with my family in the open park, laughing and running freely across the wide green field.

**B 版**

> Whenever spring arrives, I can't wait to fly colourful kites in the riverside park, which has become our family's most beloved weekend tradition.

---

## Sentence 5 · 另一活动/节日

📌 **写作指导**：引入室内活动或节日场景，用 Besides / In addition / What's more 与上句衔接，体现丰富性。

**A 版**

> Besides outdoor fun, I also truly enjoy preparing traditional spring dishes together with my grandmother, carefully wrapping each dumpling by hand.

**B 版**

> What's more, spring is a season when our whole family gathers cosily around the table, sharing warm stories and delicious food that my grandmother prepares with love.

---

## Sentence 6 · 情感深化

📌 **写作指导**：写该季节带给你的心理感受或精神力量，用 make sb. feel 或比较级强调独特性，避免空洞。

**A 版**

> Spring always completely fills me with energy and makes me feel genuinely ready to take on any challenge bravely.

**B 版**

> No other season can refresh my mind and lift my spirits quite like spring does, which is exactly why I treasure every single moment of it.

---

## Sentence 7 · 转折让步

📌 **写作指导**：诚实承认该季节一个小缺点，随即用 however / even so 转折，体现辩证思维和真实感。

**A 版**

> Although spring can sometimes be unexpectedly rainy, I honestly don't mind at all because the refreshing smell of wet earth afterwards completely makes up for it.

**B 版**

> Admittedly, spring brings occasional rainy days that may seem disappointing, but the fresh, clean world that follows them, which sparkles with raindrops, far outweighs any discomfort.

---

## Sentence 8 · 具体回忆

📌 **写作指导**：用过去时讲一次个人亲身经历（某天/某年），有起因结果和情感，使文章生动立体。

**A 版**

> I still remember clearly one spring afternoon when I discovered a hidden garden full of butterflies, and I stood there silently, completely lost in its magical beauty.

**B 版**

> Once, I spent an entire afternoon wandering through a cherry blossom garden near my home, which turned out to be the most peaceful and unforgettable moment of my entire spring.

---

## Sentence 9 · 升华感悟

📌 **写作指导**：由个人经历上升到普遍意义——季节对人的启示或人与自然的关系，提升立意层次。

**A 版**

> Spring gently teaches me to appreciate the simple beauty of nature and patiently reminds me that life always renews itself after every cold winter.

**B 版**

> I truly believe that spring is nature's most beautiful way of reminding us to slow down, breathe deeply, and notice the tiny wonders that surround us every single day.

---

## Sentence 10 · 结论收尾

📌 **写作指导**：呼应开头，换新词汇重申主旨，加展望或邀请读者共鸣，结尾有力不拖沓。

**A 版**

> To sum up, spring is far more than just a season to me — it is a yearly reminder to live hopefully and embrace every fresh beginning wholeheartedly.

**B 版**

> That is why, year after year, I find myself counting down the days until spring returns, bringing with it a world of colour, warmth, and endless possibilities.

---

# 🤝 Topic 2 · My Best Friend {#topic2}

**年份**：2022仿写 | **主题**：介绍最好的朋友及你们的友谊

## Opening 开篇句

> Everyone needs a good friend in life.

## Sentence 2 · 引出人物

📌 **写作指导**：介绍朋友姓名+年龄+一个最鲜明的外貌或性格特征，用 who/whose 定语从句增加信息量，避免流水账。

**A 版**

> My best friend is Li Hua, a cheerful and thoughtful boy whose warm smile immediately puts everyone around him completely at ease.

**B 版**

> I have a best friend called Li Hua, whose bright eyes and ready smile, which never fail to light up any room, make him the kindest person I have ever known.

---

## Sentence 3 · 性格特点

📌 **写作指导**：写2-3个性格词，每个紧接具体行为表现（he always / she never），让性格可感可信。

**A 版**

> What I admire most deeply about Li Hua is that he always listens patiently to others, even when he is busy himself.

**B 版**

> Li Hua is the most generous and honest person I know, who never hesitates to share whatever he has with friends in need, which has earned him everyone's trust and respect.

---

## Sentence 4 · 共同爱好

📌 **写作指导**：写你们共有的兴趣，用 both...and 或 share a passion for，强调 "共同" 而非单独描写一人。

**A 版**

> We both love playing basketball passionately and often spend entire afternoons together on the court, completely losing track of time.

**B 版**

> We share a deep passion for basketball, which has brought us countless unforgettable afternoons of laughter, teamwork, and friendly competition on the sunny court.

---

## Sentence 5 · 难忘经历

📌 **写作指导**：过去时讲一次具体的共同经历：有背景、转折和情感反应。It was...who 强调句可提分。

**A 版**

> I clearly remember the time when I failed an important exam, and it was Li Hua who sat beside me quietly, encouraging me patiently until I smiled again.

**B 版**

> Once, when I felt completely defeated by a terrible exam score, Li Hua stayed with me for hours, sharing his own past struggles and reminding me that failure is just a stepping stone to success.

---

## Sentence 6 · 朋友的影响

📌 **写作指导**：写朋友如何改变或提升了你，用 Thanks to / Because of 或现在完成时表持续影响。

**A 版**

> Thanks to his patient encouragement, I have gradually become more confident and learned to face challenges bravely instead of giving up.

**B 版**

> His constant support and wise advice, which have never failed to guide me through difficult moments, have gradually shaped me into a stronger and more hopeful person.

---

## Sentence 7 · 真友谊的内涵

📌 **写作指导**：上升到你对"真正友谊"的理解，用一个比喻或转折，展示思想深度，避免口号化。

**A 版**

> True friendship, I have slowly come to understand, is not about always agreeing with each other, but about standing firmly by each other when things get difficult.

**B 版**

> A true friend, I believe, is like a warm lighthouse on a dark night — someone who guides you safely home, whose steady light never dims even in the stormiest weather.

---

## Sentence 8 · 现状近况

📌 **写作指导**：交代目前联系方式或近况，时态从过去切换回现在，体现时态灵活运用。

**A 版**

> Although we now attend different schools, we still stay closely in touch, regularly calling each other to share our latest news and laugh together.

**B 版**

> To this day, we remain as close as ever, chatting every evening about everything from school life to our wildest dreams, which keeps our friendship fresh and strong.

---

## Sentence 9 · 展望/心愿

📌 **写作指导**：表达对未来友谊的期盼，用 I hope / I believe / I look forward to，加具体、不泛泛的内容。

**A 版**

> I sincerely hope that we will continue supporting each other through every stage of life and bravely face whatever challenges the future may bring together.

**B 版**

> I firmly believe that our friendship, which has already survived distance and time, will only grow deeper as we journey through life, sharing both our triumphs and our struggles.

---

## Sentence 10 · 结论点题

📌 **写作指导**：有力收尾，换词呼应开头，可用排比或升华句，让读者记住你对友谊的独特理解。

**A 版**

> In a word, Li Hua is not simply my best friend — he is the brother life generously gave me, someone who has made my world richer and far more meaningful.

**B 版**

> Without doubt, having a friend like Li Hua — someone who laughs with you in joy and stands beside you in darkness — is the most precious gift that life has ever offered me.

---

# 🎪 Topic 3 · A School Activity I Enjoyed {#topic3}

**年份**：2024仿写 | **主题**：描述一次印象深刻的校园活动

## Opening 开篇句

> School life is filled with wonderful activities.

## Sentence 2 · 点题引出

📌 **写作指导**：点明活动名称、时间、地点，用定语从句让句子信息丰富。避免只说 "We had an activity"——要具体。

**A 版**

> One activity that deeply impressed me was the school talent show, carefully organised by our student council last spring.

**B 版**

> Last spring, our school organised a talent show that turned out to be one of the most exciting and memorable events, which brought together students from every grade.

---

## Sentence 3 · 活动背景/准备

📌 **写作指导**：描写活动前的期待与准备，用过去完成时或过去进行时体现时间层次。

**A 版**

> We had eagerly looked forward to this event for weeks, spending nearly every lunch break practising our performance together in the empty music room.

**B 版**

> Before the big day arrived, our entire class had thrown ourselves into weeks of intense preparation, which filled the rehearsal room with nervous laughter, forgotten lines, and growing excitement.

---

## Sentence 4 · 活动过程①

📌 **写作指导**：按时间顺序描写活动第一阶段，First / To begin with 衔接，加一个具体的感官描写细节。

**A 版**

> To begin with, the school hall was completely filled with excited students and teachers, and the air buzzed warmly with anticipation and cheerful chatter.

**B 版**

> First, the lights dimmed slowly and a hush fell over the packed hall — a magical silence, which was soon broken by the opening notes of music that sent a shiver of excitement through the crowd.

---

## Sentence 5 · 活动过程②/高潮

📌 **写作指导**：写最精彩的高潮时刻，用 Suddenly / At that moment / To everyone's surprise 制造转折感和惊喜。

**A 版**

> Suddenly, a shy boy from our class stepped confidently onto the stage and sang so beautifully that the entire audience fell completely silent before bursting into wild applause.

**B 版**

> To everyone's complete surprise, the quietest student in our grade delivered a breathtaking piano performance, which turned the ordinary afternoon into an unforgettable moment that moved many to tears.

---

## Sentence 6 · 个人参与

📌 **写作指导**：写你自己在活动中的具体角色或表现，第一人称细节使叙述有代入感，加情感动词。

**A 版**

> I was responsible for introducing each act, and although my hands were shaking slightly, I spoke clearly and managed to keep the audience engaged throughout the show.

**B 版**

> When it was finally my turn to present our class project, I walked onto the brightly lit stage with a pounding heart, but the warm smiles and encouraging nods from my classmates, which I spotted in the front row, gave me instant courage.

---

## Sentence 7 · 同伴反应/合作

📌 **写作指导**：写同学或老师的反应，体现集体意义，间接引语或描写性句子增加真实感和温度。

**A 版**

> My classmates cheered loudly from the front row, waving their hands excitedly, and our teacher nodded approvingly with tears of pride in her eyes.

**B 版**

> Our teacher later told us with a trembling voice that she had never felt prouder, and her heartfelt words, which echoed in the quiet hall, gave every one of us the strength to believe in ourselves more deeply.

---

## Sentence 8 · 情感收获

📌 **写作指导**：写活动后你的精神和情感收获，用 I realised / I discovered / not only...but also 体现成长。

**A 版**

> After the show, I suddenly realised that courage is not about being fearless, but about stepping forward bravely even when your heart is racing with fear.

**B 版**

> I gained not only a surge of confidence but also a deep understanding of what it truly means to work as one team — a lesson that has stayed with me long after the applause faded.

---

## Sentence 9 · 活动的意义

📌 **写作指导**：将个人经历升华为对校园活动普遍价值的思考，Such activities... 引出，体现大局观。

**A 版**

> Such activities genuinely allow us to discover hidden talents and gradually build confidence that cannot be learned from textbooks or exams alone.

**B 版**

> Events like this talent show, which pull us out of our daily routine and push us into the spotlight, teach us invaluable lessons about courage and self-belief that no classroom alone can offer.

---

## Sentence 10 · 结论/期盼

📌 **写作指导**：以积极向上的语气结尾，期望更多类似活动，首尾呼应，可用 make + 宾 + 形容词收束。

**A 版**

> I sincerely hope our school will continue organising more events like this, for they genuinely make our school life richer, more colourful, and truly unforgettable.

**B 版**

> Wonderful activities like this constantly remind me that school is not just a place for grades and exams — it is also where we discover who we really are, surrounded by people who believe in us.

---

# 🏙️ Topic 4 · My Hometown {#topic4}

**年份**：2021仿写 | **主题**：介绍家乡的特色与变化

## Opening 开篇句

> I come from a city called Wuhan, which is known as 'the city of rivers'.

## Sentence 2 · 地理位置

📌 **写作指导**：介绍家乡的地理位置或自然环境，用 located / situated / which 引出，简洁而信息丰富。

**A 版**

> Located right in the heart of central China, my hometown is a vibrant city where the mighty Yangtze River flows gracefully through its very centre.

**B 版**

> My hometown Wuhan, which is often called the city of rivers, sits proudly at the meeting point of the Yangtze and Han rivers, creating a landscape of shimmering water and endless bridges.

---

## Sentence 3 · 著名景点

📌 **写作指导**：介绍一个最具代表性的景点，用 where / which 定语从句补充信息，避免只列名称不描述。

**A 版**

> One of its most famous attractions is the Yellow Crane Tower, standing majestically on a hilltop where visitors can look out over the entire city and the flowing river below.

**B 版**

> The most iconic landmark is the Yellow Crane Tower, a magnificent ancient pagoda whose golden roof catches the morning sun, which attracts millions of visitors who come to admire its timeless beauty.

---

## Sentence 4 · 美食文化

📌 **写作指导**：写家乡特色美食，用具体名称和口感描写，让读者"尝"到家乡味道，体现文化自信。

**A 版**

> When it comes to food, I can never resist the famous hot dry noodles, generously mixed with sesame paste and spices, filling the morning air with their rich, inviting smell.

**B 版**

> Nothing can compare to Wuhan's hot dry noodles, a simple yet unforgettable dish whose chewy texture and nutty sesame aroma, which hits you the moment you walk into any street stall, make it the soul of our city's breakfast culture.

---

## Sentence 5 · 历史文化

📌 **写作指导**：谈家乡的历史或文化底蕴，用 has a history of / dates back to / is deeply rooted in，展示深度。

**A 版**

> My hometown has a history of over 3,500 years and is proudly home to the ancient Chu culture, whose art and poetry have deeply influenced Chinese civilisation.

**B 版**

> Wuhan's culture is deeply rooted in centuries of river trade and poetry, with countless traditions and legends, which have been lovingly preserved and passed down through countless generations of proud Wuhan people.

---

## Sentence 6 · 近年变化

📌 **写作指导**：写家乡近年来的发展变化，用现在完成时（have/has + 过去分词）体现持续变化。

**A 版**

> In recent years, Wuhan has rapidly transformed into a modern metropolis, with gleaming skyscrapers rising everywhere and efficient metro lines connecting every corner of the city.

**B 版**

> Over the past decade, my hometown has changed beyond recognition, becoming a stunning blend of old and new where centuries-old temples stand peacefully beside towering glass buildings, which perfectly captures Wuhan's unique spirit.

---

## Sentence 7 · 个人情感

📌 **写作指导**：抒发你对家乡的个人情感，用 whenever / no matter where 强调情感深度，避免干燥叙述。

**A 版**

> Whenever I think of my hometown, I instantly feel a deep and warm sense of belonging — a feeling that no other city has ever been able to give me.

**B 版**

> No matter where life takes me in the future, Wuhan will always be the place where my fondest memories live — the familiar streets, the morning market sounds, and the river breeze that carries the scent of home.

---

## Sentence 8 · 与众不同之处

📌 **写作指导**：点出家乡最独特、最难忘的一点，用 What makes...special is that 结构突出重点，提升表达档次。

**A 版**

> What truly makes Wuhan special is that it never stops moving forward — people here work tirelessly, dream boldly, and warmly welcome everyone who comes to this city.

**B 版**

> What sets Wuhan apart from every other city I have visited is its people's unbreakable spirit — a rare combination of toughness and warmth, which you can feel in every bowl of noodles served with a smile and every helping hand offered to a stranger.

---

## Sentence 9 · 对家乡的期望

📌 **写作指导**：展望家乡未来，用 I hope / I believe / I am confident that 表达美好愿景，积极正向。

**A 版**

> I firmly believe that Wuhan will continue growing steadily, offering more opportunities for young people and proudly remaining a city where dreams can truly take flight.

**B 版**

> I am deeply confident that my hometown, which has already achieved so much against all odds, will grow into an even more prosperous and inclusive city that future generations will be proud to call home.

---

## Sentence 10 · 结论收尾

📌 **写作指导**：真情实感收尾，回应开头"我来自武汉"，用排比或升华句让结尾令人印象深刻。

**A 版**

> In a word, Wuhan is not simply a city on a map to me — it is my roots, my pride, and the place that has quietly shaped me into the person I am today.

**B 版**

> Above all, no matter how far I travel or how much I change over the years, Wuhan — this loud, warm, unstoppable city of rivers — will forever remain the place where my heart truly belongs.

---

# 🎨 Topic 5 · My Hobby {#topic5}

**年份**：2020仿写 | **主题**：介绍你的爱好及它对你的影响

## Opening 开篇句

> Having a hobby can make our life more colourful and meaningful.

## Sentence 2 · 点明爱好

📌 **写作指导**：直接点明你的爱好，加 ever since / as long as I can remember 说明爱好的持久性，体现真实热爱。

**A 版**

> Ever since I was a little child, I have been deeply passionate about painting, spending countless quiet hours completely absorbed in creating worlds with colours and brushes.

**B 版**

> My greatest passion is painting, which I have loved for as long as I can remember — a quiet, magical activity that transforms a blank white canvas into a window of endless imagination.

---

## Sentence 3 · 爱好起源

📌 **写作指导**：讲述你是如何开始这个爱好的——某人的影响或某次经历，过去时叙事，有起因有细节。

**A 版**

> It all started when my grandfather quietly placed a worn paintbrush in my tiny hand and patiently showed me how to paint my very first flower on a sunny afternoon.

**B 版**

> I first fell in love with painting after watching my grandfather, a quiet man whose rough hands could create the most delicate watercolour landscapes, which seemed to breathe with life on the paper.

---

## Sentence 4 · 具体描写①

📌 **写作指导**：描写你进行这项爱好时的具体动作和感受，用现在时或现在进行时，让读者身临其境。

**A 版**

> Whenever I sit down to paint, I completely lose myself in the rhythm of brushstrokes, carefully mixing colours and quietly watching shapes emerge slowly on the blank paper.

**B 版**

> As soon as I open my paint box and pick up a brush, the noisy world outside fades into silence, and I enter a peaceful, colourful universe where time seems to stand perfectly still.

---

## Sentence 5 · 具体描写②

📌 **写作指导**：从另一角度描写这个爱好，写一次具体成果或进步，体现努力和成长，过去时或完成时。

**A 版**

> After months of practising patiently, I finally completed my first detailed painting of our garden, and I felt so genuinely proud that I hung it on my wall immediately.

**B 版**

> I remember the unforgettable moment when I finished my first landscape painting — an imperfect yet beautiful scene of sunset over East Lake, which filled me with such a powerful sense of achievement that I knew painting would be part of my life forever.

---

## Sentence 6 · 爱好的益处

📌 **写作指导**：写爱好给你带来的具体益处（不止一点），用 not only...but also 并列，展示多角度思维。

**A 版**

> Painting not only helps me relieve stress after a long school day, but it has also gradually taught me patience and the quiet power of focusing deeply on one thing at a time.

**B 版**

> My hobby has brought me far more than just beautiful pictures — it has given me patience when things go wrong, creativity when ideas run dry, and a peaceful corner of life that no exam or test can ever disturb.

---

## Sentence 7 · 与他人分享

📌 **写作指导**：写你与朋友或家人分享这个爱好的经历，体现爱好的社交价值，用间接引语或描写。

**A 版**

> I have happily shared my love of painting with my best friend, who initially knew nothing about art but gradually became just as passionate as I am.

**B 版**

> My mother once told me, with tears glistening in her eyes, that watching me paint so wholeheartedly reminded her of her own forgotten dreams, which inspired her to pick up a brush again after thirty years.

---

## Sentence 8 · 克服困难

📌 **写作指导**：诚实写出过程中遇到的困难以及如何克服，展示毅力和成长，no matter how 让步结构可加分。

**A 版**

> There were countless times when I wanted to throw my brush down in frustration, but I stubbornly kept going because every failed painting silently taught me something new.

**B 版**

> No matter how frustrating it felt to ruin a painting I had worked on for hours, I always reminded myself that even the greatest artists started somewhere — a truth, which I now carry with me whenever I face any difficulty in life.

---

## Sentence 9 · 爱好的更深意义

📌 **写作指导**：从个人爱好升华到对生活态度的影响，I have come to realise / I now believe 体现成长式思考。

**A 版**

> I have slowly come to realise that painting is far more than just a hobby — it is a quiet way to understand myself more deeply and see the hidden beauty in ordinary moments.

**B 版**

> Painting has become, to me, far more than just a pastime — it is a lens through which I now view the world, a quiet teacher whose lessons about patience, persistence, and finding beauty in imperfection have quietly reshaped who I am.

---

## Sentence 10 · 结论收尾
